Our Services

Specialist Bat Survey Support

From field surveys to data analysis, we provide the specialist capacity your consultancy needs.

Bat Surveys (PRA, Emergence & Return, Activity)

Consultancies needing additional surveyor capacity or full bat survey packages.

Deliverables: PRA reports, emergence/return reports, activity summaries, mitigation recommendations.

Turnaround: PRA within 5 working days. Emergence/return within 10 working days.

Price: From £350 (PRA) / £500 per survey visit.

Static Detector Acoustic Analysis

Expert species identification and analysis for static detector datasets.

Deliverables: Confidence-rated species ID, summary tables, nightly pass counts, QA-checked outputs.

Turnaround: Typically 5–10 working days.

Price: From £150 per detector-month.

Thermal & Video Review

Ecologist-led review and behaviour documentation from infrared/video surveys.

Deliverables: Timestamped behaviour logs, annotated clips, summary report.

Turnaround: 5–7 working days per survey night.

Price: From £200 per survey night.

Data & Process Support

Standardise survey workflows, improve QA processes, and build internal capacity.

Deliverables: Templates, QA frameworks, SOP/training materials, process recommendations.

Price: From £500 per engagement.

Subcontract Surveyor Support

Licensed surveyor support during peak season (May–September).

Deliverables: Surveyors, equipment support, field notes, post-survey debrief.

Price: Day rates from £300 (travel may apply).
