What We Do

Specialist Bat Services

From fieldwork to data processing, we provide the support your consultancy needs to deliver high-quality bat survey outputs.

Building and tree inspections for bat roost potential. Clear grading and actionable recommendations.

Emergence & Return Surveys

Dusk/dawn survey support with experienced surveyors. Full reporting and species ID.

Static Detector Analysis

Thermal / Video Review

Ecologist-led review of infrared and video footage with behaviour tagging and annotation.
